Chapel of Anticipation Part 1

  • If you pre-ordered, then you will receive The Ring gesture.
  • Loot Tarnished's Wizened Finger from the body.
  • Die to the Grafted Scion to teleport to Stranded Graveyard.

Stranded Graveyard Part 1

  • Upon waking up, receive Flask of Crimson Tears and Flask of Cerulean Tears.
  • Jump down and get Cave of Knowledge Site of Grace.
  • Defeat Soldier of Godrick.
  • Head straight and collect the Strength! gesture.
  • Head up the stairs and open the door to get the Stranded Graveyard Site of Grace.
  • By the archway, on the right, loot Finger Severer and Tarnished's Furled Finger.
  • Go outside to enter Limgrave.

Limgrave Part 1

  • Get The First Step Site of Grace.
  • Exhaust dialogue with White Mask Varre.
  • Head to Church of Elleh.

Church of Elleh Part 1

  • Get the Church of Elleh Site of Grace.
  • Exhaust dialogue with Merchant Kale.
  • Head to Gatefront Ruins.

Gatefront Ruins Part 1

  • Get the Gatefront Ruins Site of Grace.
  • Exhaust dialogue with Melina to receive Spectral Steed Whistle.
  • Open the chest on the northern Hearse to get Lordsworn's Greatsword.
  • Open the chest on the other nearby Hearse to get Flail.
  • Go by the center pillar and loot Map: Limgrave, West.
  • Go underground and open the chest to get Whetstone Knife.
  • Go underground and open the chest to get Ash of War: Storm Stomp.
  • Get the nearby Agheel Lake North Site of Grace.
  • Head to Stranded Graveyard.

Stranded Graveyard Part 2

  • Get Seaside Ruins Site of Grace.
  • Enter Stranded Graveyard from the cave on the south-east beach to get Haligdrake Talisman.
  • Teleport to Church of Elleh.

Church of Elleh Part 2

  • Exhaust dialogue with Renna to get Lone Wolf Ashes.
  • Exhaust dialogue with Renna to get Spirit Calling Bell.
  • Purchase Telescope from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase 3x Cracked Pot from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Crafting Kit from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [1] from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [2] from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Missionary's Cookbook [1] from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Torch from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Large Leather Shield from Merchant Kale if you did not select the Hero class.
  • Purchase Chain Coif from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Chain Armor from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Chain Gauntlets from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Chain Leggings from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Note: Flask of Wondrous Physick from Merchant Kale.
  • Purchase Note: Waypoint Ruins from Merchant Kale.
  • Teleport to Agheel Lake North Site of Grace.

Limgrave Part 2

  • Hit the talking tree next to the telescope on the map and exhaust dialogue with Boc the Seamster.
  • Head to Coastal Cave.

Coastal Cave Part 1

  • Get the Coastal Cave Site of Grace.
  • Exhaust dialogue with Boc the Seamster.
  • Defeat the Demi-Human Chief.
  • Get the Tailoring Tools from the Demi-Human Chief.
  • Get the Sewing Needle from the Demi-Human Chief.
  • Go through the cave and get the Church of Dragon Communion Site of Grace.
  • Teleport to the Coastal Cave Site of Grace and give Boc the Seamster the Sewing Needle key item.
  • Outside the cave, go east and exhaust dialogue with Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave.
  • Purchase Armorer's Cookbook [2] from Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave.
  • Purchase Broadsword from Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave if you did not select the Confessor class.
  • Purchase Iron Roundshield from Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave.
  • Purchase Note: Land Squirts from Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave.
  • Purchase Note: Stonedigger Trolls from Nomadic Merchant West Limgrave.
  • Teleport to the Church of Dragon Communion Site of Grace.

Church of Dragon Communion Part 1

  • Purchase the Dragonclaw incantation from the church altar at night.
  • Purchase the Dragonfire incantation from the church altar at night.
  • Purchase the Dragonmaw incantation from the church altar at night.

FAQ

The foundation of this guide was based on Stephen McNabb's Dark Soul 2 Cheat Sheet source code and DeathGodGarra's NPC Side Quests Guide V2 walkthrough guide

This guide has evolved thanks to the many GitHub contributors with many great suggestions, fixes and improvements!

I have feedback, how can I contribute?

You can visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request.

Is this a guide?

Nope, not really. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing Dark Souls to make sure you don't miss an item, action, conversation, or boss. If you need playthroughs then you can click on the zone titles to read the wiki walkthrough.

Do I need to follow the playthrough in this exact order?

Dark Souls 3 is not a linear game and has multiple ways of progressing. This guide highlights zones as they become available, so feel free to follow however you please. You can always check things off in a different order.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons in the options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles.

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
